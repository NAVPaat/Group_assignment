About Us

It all started out during the year 2022, Firdaus (CEO of Bantai) realized that there weren't really any Gundam Model Kits that was sold in Malaysia Market. Even in Hobby Shops, there were not many selection of Gundam Model Kits.


Knowing that Gundam was such a famous and Iconic Anime, Firdaus are sure there is a lot of people or fans from Malaysia who are interested in collecting Gundam Model Kits but the issue was it was hard to get our hands on it especially for those who were from rural areas.


Hence, Firdaus and his friends have decided to get sources from local and overseas suppliers to start selling online and that's how Bantai was born. The company began from instead of just selling Gundam plastic model kits, now Bantai has added 2 new types of models to the sale, namely Star Wars and Digimon.

Objectives

  • Fulfilling the dream of everyone, regardless of age, to collect Plastic Model Kits.
  • To advocate the beautiful culture of Gunpla Modelling among teenagers.
  • To provide the most variety of Gunpla model kits at the most affordable price to the community.
